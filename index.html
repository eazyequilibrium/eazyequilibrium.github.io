Diving

A diving club is not just a place for diving, it is a community of people united by a common passion for the underwater world and the desire to explore it together. Here you can not only learn and improve your skills, but also find friends and like-minded people who are ready to share their knowledge and experience.

Friendship

Divers know that there are no borders and language barriers underwater. The world under water unites people, regardless of their origin and cultural differences. Friendship between divers is something special that turns ordinary dives into unforgettable adventures and helps to overcome all difficulties, both on land and underwater.

Training

Diver training is not only a preparation for physical activity and learning new skills, but also a way to develop confidence and mastery underwater. Training helps you overcome yourself and improve your results in order to get the most out of every dive and feel in your element.

Trainer

A good diving coach is not only a mentor, but also a friend and partner who is ready to support and help in any situation underwater. It gives not only knowledge and skills, but also confidence and motivation to reach new heights underwater and in life in general.

For recreational diving, there are usually no hard weight limits, as diving uses equipment that allows you to compensate for buoyancy. However, if a person has medical contraindications or restrictions related to weight or health condition, then he should receive appropriate advice from a doctor and diving instructor before starting classes.
